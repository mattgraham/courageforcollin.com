    Collin's Story

    Collin Vani is a 16 year old native of Port Huron, MI and student at Port Huron High School who was recently diagnosed with Clear Cell Sarcoma of the Kidney. Days after the discovery had surgery to have one of his kidneys removed. He is currently in the long battle with chemotherapy and radiation treatments to ensure that we have battled through the cancer process.

    Please pray for the recovery and healing process going on inside of Collin's body. We believe there is a good work happening in the midst of a painful season of the lives of the family. I'd also ask you to consider donating to the many expenses that the family will have during the next 6 months of travel to and from Detroit Childrens Hospital. Every bit can and will help. Lets help Collin battle.
